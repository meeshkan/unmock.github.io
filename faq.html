Frequently asked questions

Why build yet another mocking library?

See comparison to existing tools.

Why is it called unmock if it's a mocking library?

The long-term mission at unmock.io is to provide a functionally adequate mock of the internet, so you don't have to mock. This mocking library is a side-product of that.

Can I record requests and mocks?

Not yet, but soon you can! Please add feature requests in GitHub.
